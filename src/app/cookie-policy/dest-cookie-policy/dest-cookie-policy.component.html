Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on destinique.com, and how you can manage your preferences. We respect your privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to the site owners. Cookies can be “first-party” (set by us) or “third-party” (set by services we use, such as analytics or advertising providers).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable core features such as security, authentication, and session management. You cannot disable these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. which pages are viewed, how long users stay). We use this information to improve our website and services. We may use tools such as Google Analytics and Hotjar for this purpose. You can turn these on or off in your cookie preferences.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your choices (e.g. language, region) and improve your experience. They are optional and can be managed in your cookie preferences.

Marketing / Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be used to deliver relevant ads and to measure the effectiveness of campaigns. They can track activity across websites. We may use services such as Google Ads and Facebook Pixel. You can turn these on or off in your cookie preferences.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. The main third-party services we may use include:

  • Google Analytics – traffic and behavior analysis
  • Google Ads – advertising and remarketing
  • Hotjar – user behavior and feedback
  • Facebook Pixel – advertising and conversion tracking

Each of these providers has its own privacy and cookie policy. We encourage you to review their policies for more detail.

4. Storage Duration

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for a set period (e.g. up to 12 months) or until you delete them. Your cookie consent choices are stored for a set period (e.g. 6 or 12 months), after which we may ask you to confirm your preferences again.

5. How to Change Your Cookie Settings

You can update your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Use the “Manage Cookies” link in our website footer to open the cookie preferences panel.
  • From there you can turn analytics, functional, and marketing cookies on or off. Necessary cookies always remain active.
