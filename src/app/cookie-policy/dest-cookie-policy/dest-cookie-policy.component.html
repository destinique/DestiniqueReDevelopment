Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on destinique.com, and how you can manage your preferences. We comply with applicable data protection laws and respect your privacy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help sites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners. Cookies can be first-party (set by us) or third-party (set by services we use, such as analytics or advertising providers).

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Necessary

    Always on

    Essential for the website to function. They enable security, authentication, and session management. These cannot be disabled.

  • Analytics

    Optional

    Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g. which pages are viewed, how long users stay). We may use tools such as Google Analytics and Hotjar. You can turn these on or off in your cookie preferences.

  • Functional

    Optional

    Remember your choices (e.g. language, region) and improve your experience. They can be managed in your cookie preferences.

  • Marketing / advertising

    Optional

    Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness. They can track activity across websites. We may use services such as Google Ads and Facebook Pixel. You can turn these on or off in your cookie preferences.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Services we may use include:

  • Google Analytics – traffic and behavior analysis
  • Google Ads – advertising and remarketing
  • Hotjar – user behavior and feedback
  • Facebook Pixel – advertising and conversion tracking

Each provider has its own privacy and cookie policy. We encourage you to review their policies for more detail.

4. Storage duration

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain for a set period (e.g. up to 12 months) or until you delete them. Your consent choices are stored for a set period (e.g. 6 or 12 months), after which we may ask you to confirm your preferences again.

5. How to change your cookie settings

You can update your preferences at any time:

  • Use the “Manage cookies” or “Cookie preferences” link in our website footer to open the cookie preferences panel.
  • From there you can turn analytics, functional, and marketing cookies on or off. Necessary cookies always remain active.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or our use of cookies, please contact us at inquiry@destinique.com.
