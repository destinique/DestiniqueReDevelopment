Destinique Travel Cookie Policy

Destinique Travel uses cookies and similar technologies to operate our website, improve performance, understand site usage, and support marketing activities.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information about how visitors interact with the site.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Necessary Cookies

    Always Active

    These cookies are required for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

  • Analytics Cookies

    Optional

    These cookies help us understand how visitors use the website so we can improve functionality and performance.

    Examples may include:
    • _ga
    • _gid

  • Functional Cookies

    Optional

    These cookies allow the website to remember preferences and personalize your experience.
    Some functional cookies may be treated as necessary depending on implementation.

  • Marketing Cookies

    Optional

    These cookies are used for advertising, tracking, remarketing, and sharing data with third parties.
    This may be considered “sharing” under California law.

    Examples may include:
    • _fbp
    • _gcl_au

3. How Long Do Cookies Stay?

  • Session Cookies

    These are temporary and expire once you close your browser.

  • Persistent Cookies

    These remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Operate and secure the website
  • Improve functionality and performance
  • Understand website usage
  • Support advertising and marketing efforts where permitted

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize cookie preferences

You may update your preferences at any time through the "Manage Cookies" link on our website.

6. Third-Party Cookies and Similar Technologies

Some cookies and similar technologies used on our website are placed by third parties, including analytics and advertising partners such as Google and Meta.

👉 These third parties may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites and services, subject to your consent where required.

👉 We do not control these third-party technologies, and their use is governed by the privacy policies of those third parties.

👉 We may share limited data with our service providers and host agency systems solely for the purpose of delivering and improving our services.

7. Managing Your Preferences

We use a consent management platform that allows you to accept, reject, or customize your cookie preferences before cookies are set.

👉 Non-essential cookies are blocked by default and are only activated after you provide your consent through our consent management platform.

👉 You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time through the “Manage Cookies” link available on our website.

👉 You may also configure your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality and performance of the website.

  • Use the “Manage cookies” or “Manage cookie preferences” link in our website footer to open the cookie preferences panel.
  • From there you can turn analytics, functional, and marketing cookies on or off. Necessary cookies always remain active.

If your browser sends a Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal, it will be treated as a request to reject non-essential cookies.
