Destinique Travel Privacy Policy

Destinique Travel is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is used.

1. Information we collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone number
  • Travel details and preferences
  • Information submitted through forms, inquiries, or bookings

If payment information is provided, payments may be processed through secure third-party providers or Suppliers.

B. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Pages visited
  • Referral source
  • Site interactions

This information is collected using cookies and similar technologies, only where permitted or with your consent.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide travel advisory and booking services
  • Coordinate bookings with Suppliers
  • Customize recommendations
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send communications where permitted

3. How We Share Information

We share information only as necessary to provide services or operate our business.

This may include:

A. Travel Providers

  • Hotels
  • Villa providers
  • Property managers
  • Tour operators
  • Transportation providers

B. Business Platforms

  • Booking systems
  • Host agency systems
  • Preferred supplier networks, including Virtuoso-affiliated suppliers where applicable

C. Technology Providers

  • Website hosting providers
  • Analytics providers
  • CRM and communication tools
  • Marketing and advertising platforms (where consent is provided if required)

D. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. California Privacy Rights (CPRA)

Certain sharing of data with advertising partners may be considered “sharing” under California law.

You may opt out of this sharing through:

  • Cookie preferences
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC)

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your information
  • Request correction or deletion where applicable
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage cookie preferences

7. Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We recognize Global Privacy Control signals and treat them as a request to limit non-essential tracking technologies.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for business, operational, and legal purposes.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

11. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
